# About me

Oscar Nierstrasz is Professor emeritus of Computer Science at the [University of Bern](http://www.unibe.ch/index_eng.html), where he founded the [Software Composition Group](https://scg.unibe.ch) in 1994.
He retired from the University at the end of 2021, and is currently working at [feenk.com](https://feenk.com/about/).
He is co-author of over 400 [publications](/publications), including the open-source books *[Object-Oriented Reengineering Patterns](https://scg.unibe.ch/assets/download/oorp/)* and *[Pharo by Example](http://books.pharo.org)*.
For more information about his former academic activities, please see his [old SCG home page](https://scg.unibe.ch/legacy/oscar).
